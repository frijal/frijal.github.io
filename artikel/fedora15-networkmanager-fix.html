Fedora15 NetworkManagerFix ModemUSB/Ponsel
Kalem,modemUSBmacethabisupdate?Kitabetulinbareng AdabugdipaketNetworkManagertanggal26Mei2011yangbikinkoneksiselulergagal.Solusinya:pasangbuildperbaikan,bersihkankoneksilama,danstartfresh.

Bug:gnome-0.8.999.3.git20110526.fc15• Fix:NetworkManager-gnome-0.8.9997-1.git20110531.fc15 Garisbesarnya:UnduhpaketRPMyangdibutuhin, installbarengviaterminal,hapusprofilelamadisystem-connections, lalujalankanNetworkManagerlagi.Selesai,modemUSBdantethering ponselbalikwaras.

Peringatansingkat:Sebelummenghapusisi /etc/NetworkManager/system-connections,backupdulufoldernya.Contoh: sudocp-a/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections{,.backup}

LangkahringkasviaTerminal

Kitapakaiterminalbiarrapidanbisaditelusuri.Pastikankoneksiinternetaktif(pakaikabel/Wi‑Fi).SimpansemuaRPMdisatufolder.

1.Unduhpaketyangdiperlukan

mkdir-p~/Downloads/nm-fix&&cd~/Downloads/nm-fix#Dependensi(contohi686sesuaisumberlama)#dbus,glib2,dbus-glibdevel#PaketNetworkManagerversi0.8.9997-1.git20110531.fc15#(gunakantautanasliyangkamupunya/arsiplokal)

Daftartautanhistoris(arsip):

  • dbus-devel-1.4.6-4.fc15.i686.rpm
  • glib2-devel-2.28.6-2.fc15.i686.rpm
  • dbus-glib-devel-0.92-2.fc15.i686.rpm
  • NetworkManager-0.8.9997
  • NetworkManager-glib-0.8.9997
  • NetworkManager-devel-0.8.9997
  • NetworkManager-gnome-0.8.9997
  • NetworkManager-glib-devel-0.8.9997
  • NetworkManager-debuginfo-0.8.9997

2.InstalmassalRPMlokal

cd~/Downloads/nm-fix
sudoyumlocalinstall*.rpm

3.Bersihkanprofilkoneksilama(backupdulu!)

#Backupprofilkoneksi
sudocp-a/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections{,.backup}
#Hapusprofillama(akandibuatulang)
cd/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections/
sudorm-f*

4.MulaiulangNetworkManager

#OpsiA(service)
sudoserviceNetworkManagerrestart
#OpsiB(systemd,kalauada)
sudosystemctlrestartNetworkManager
#OpsiC(jalaninbinerlangsung-tidakselaludiperlukan)
#sudoNetworkManager
Hasilyangdiharapkan:AppletNMmunculnormal, modemUSB/ponselterdeteksi,kamubisabuatkoneksibaru,dan internetjalanlagi.Kalautetapgagal,restoreprofil: sudorm-rf/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections/*&&sudocp-a/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections.backup/*/etc/NetworkManager/system-connections/ lalurestartNM.
